THE FOUNDER

Ikheloa Omuekpen Promise

As a Registered Nurse and dedicated Philanthropist, I have witnessed first-hand the profound need within our communities, specifically for the girl child. At Ikheloa’s Foundation, we transition from observing vulnerability to delivering solutions.

My medical background informs our structured approach: provide health, secure education, and foster resilience. It is not just about charity; it is about building sustainable systems of support.

"Helping the vulnerable isn't just my slang; it is my assignment."
HOW WE WORK

Our Pillars of Intervention

🩺

Medical Outreach

Providing essential health screenings, vaccinations, and sexual health education to vulnerable girls.

🎓

Educational Aid

Securing scholarships, school supplies, and digital literacy training to ensure long-term stability.

🤝

Mentorship

Connecting girls with professional women to provide guidance, career pathways, and confidence.
